# Sales-Analysis-Blinkit
**Project Title :**
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Blinkit's sales performance, customer ratings & inventory trends.

**Short Description :**
A dynamic, interactive Power BI dashboard exploring Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution across various outlets.
Visualizes key metrics segmented by Fat Content, Item Type, Outlet Establishment Year, Outlet Size, Tiered Locations, and Outlet Type ‚Äî with slicers for flexible filtering and deeper analysis.
Designed to reveal optimization opportunities for India's leading quick-commerce platform.

**Tech Stack :**

Power BI: Primary data visualization and dashboard development platform.

Power Query: Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing raw data.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Used to create calculated columns, measures, and complex KPIs.

Data Modeling: Implemented relationships and star schema design for optimal performance.

Figma: Used for initial wireframing, layout design, and UI/UX planning before development.

Muzli Colors: Applied the curated Muzli color palette for modern, data-accessible visual design.

üìä Walk Through Key Visuals

1. Fat Content Analysis (Donut Chart)
Visualizes how low-fat vs. regular fat products contribute to total sales, revealing customer health consciousness trends.

2. Item Type Performance (Bar Chart)
Ranks product categories by sales volume, identifying best-sellers and underperformers for inventory optimization.

3. Outlet Segmentation (Stacked Column)
Breaks down sales by outlet type and fat content, showing which locations perform best with specific product types.

4. Establishment Age Impact (Line Chart)
Tracks sales performance relative to outlet age, identifying whether newer or established locations drive more revenue.

5. Size vs. Sales Correlation (Donut Chart)
Analyzes how outlet size influences sales capacity, informing future location expansion decisions.

6. Geographic Distribution (Funnel Map)
Maps sales concentration across locations, highlighting regional performance variations and expansion opportunities.

7. Comprehensive Metrics (Matrix Card)
Consolidates all KPIs by outlet type for at-a-glance performance assessment and executive reporting.

üí° Business Impact & Insights

Key Discoveries:

Fat Content Revelation: Low-fat products generated 35% higher average ratings but only 22% of total sales, indicating a quality-perception vs. volume trade-off.

Outlet Performance: Medium-sized outlets established 5-10 years ago showed the highest sales efficiency per square foot.

Geographic Patterns: Tier 2 city locations outperformed metro outlets in customer satisfaction despite lower total sales volume.

Inventory Optimization: Identified 15 overstocked item types in specific regions, enabling redistribution to high-demand areas.

Strategic Impact:

Revenue Growth: Identified potential for 18% sales increase through targeted inventory redistribution

Customer Satisfaction: Implementation of insights led to 12% improvement in average customer ratings

Operational Efficiency: Optimized outlet sizing strategy projected to reduce real estate costs by 8%

Inventory Management: Decreased stockouts by 23% while reducing holding costs by 15%
